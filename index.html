Welcome to Coding Club!

Take your time to check out the site!

Coding Club is a club dedicated to teaching 7th and 8th graders HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the bare essential tools you'll need to develop a website. This websites has all the materials you'll need to register for the club and stay caught up with the material. This club is taught by Gayatri Rajan, Luke Trenaman and Jessica Li. Club meetings are every week, usually on Thursdays.
